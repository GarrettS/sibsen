Have I experience with any kind of massage before?
Yes, I am Thai and I grow up in the countryside where people use and get massage as healing. We are far from hospital. So when people have the problem about health, they go to a healer to massage or get herb to take care their health. One time, my brother fell down from the tree and broke his arm, he didn't go to hospital but a master use some herb for it.
Do you enjoy doing massage?
Yes, I really enjoy and happy what I am doing right now. I pay attention to everyone I touch with mindfulness and sincere mind. I don't want to say that I am good but I would like people come to try. Many of my clients get better and feel happy about my work.
What I have been studying?
Swedish, deep tissue, sport, prenatal, Thai massage. I have taken many workshop from many expert in Thailand who have been doing massage for many year.
What is your massage like and what techniques do you use?
I like to combine my massage both Western and Eastern style together especially when I work on the table with oil as Swedish and deep tissue. I do Thai massage by using all kind of my tool especaily feet to step and work on the point area. I can use my feet as my hand to massage people. The pressure can go as a client need.
